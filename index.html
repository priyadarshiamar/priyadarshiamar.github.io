I am a PhD Candidate in the Political Science and Agricultural and Applied Economics Department at the University of Wisconsin-Madison

My research research interest lies at the intersection of political economy and development economics, with a focus on South Asia. For my dissertation, I examines the how instutional rules shape political candidacy in local governments, with particular focus on the participation of minorities and historically disenfranchised groups.

Apart from my dissertation, I am working on various projects on misinformation, vot and the political effects of extreme climate in India.

I hold an M.Sc. in Economics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a B.SC in Statistics from University of Delhi.
